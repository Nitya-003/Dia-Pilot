# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We, as contributors and maintainers of **Dia-Pilot**, pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free, inclusive, and respectful experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are committed to fostering an open and welcoming environment that supports collaboration, learning, and ethical innovation in public health technology.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward others
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Taking responsibility for mistakes and learning from them
- Focusing on what is best for the community and end users

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct of any kind
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information without consent
- Any behavior that compromises safety, trust, or inclusivity

---

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including:
- GitHub repositories
- Issues, pull requests, and discussions
- Community communication channels related to this project

---

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

Appropriate action may include:
- Warning the individual
- Temporary or permanent removal from project spaces
- Rejection of contributions that violate this Code of Conduct

---

## Reporting
If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it confidentially by contacting the project maintainer:

**Maintainer:** Nitya Gosain  
**Contact:** Create a private GitHub issue or reach out through official repository communication channels.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with care, discretion, and respect.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

### 1. Correction
Community members may receive a private, written warning for minor violations.

### 2. Warning
A formal warning may be issued for repeated or serious violations.

### 3. Temporary Ban
Temporary suspension from the project community may occur for continued violations.

### 4. Permanent Ban
Severe or repeated misconduct may result in permanent removal from the community.
